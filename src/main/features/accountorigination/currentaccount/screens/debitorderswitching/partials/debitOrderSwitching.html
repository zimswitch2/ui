Simple Debit Order Switching

We're with you every step

  • Standard Bank can move all your debit orders from your old account to your new account
  • This service is optional and completely free
  • We'll call you to confirm your current debit order details, and keep you up to date on our progress by SMS

What you need to do

  • Keep money in both your old and new accounts until all your debit orders are successfully switched
  • Occasionally, a service provider will not accept our request to switch your debit order. If this happens, please contact your service provider yourself and ask for the switch
  • Once all your debit orders are switched over, you can close your old account
