Need quick access to cash for unplanned expenses? A Revolving Credit Plan (RCP) lets you borrow again up to your original loan amount as soon as you repay 15% of your loan.

Highlights

  • Get a loan from R 6 000 to R 300 000
  • After you repay 15% of the loan you can borrow again up to your original loan amount
  • Your repayment amount doesn't change, enabling you to budget effectively
  • If you need additional funds, visit your branch to increase your RCP limit

How to qualify

  • You are 18 years of age or older
  • You are a South African citizen or permanent resident
  • You earn a gross monthly income of at least R 8 000

Additional information

  • The interest rate is linked to the prime rate, which is based on your risk profile
  • When the prime interest rate changes, your minimum repayment amount remains unchanged. Only the loan period increases or decreases
  • You can apply for a Personal Loan Protection Plan, which covers your loan in full in the event of your death, permanent disability and critical illness. Further, you will be covered for a maximum of 3 monthly instalments if you lose your job

What it costs

  • Once-off initiation fee
  • Monthly service fee

What you'll need

  • Your ID book
  • Your last 3 payslips, or proof of income (up to 3 months old)
  • Proof of home address (up to 3 months old)
  • 3 months' bank statements (for non-Standard Bank customers)
