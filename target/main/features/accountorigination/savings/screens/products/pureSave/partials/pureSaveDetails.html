PureSave

PureSave is an easy means to saving. Save what you can - and gain instant access to your money when you need it.

PureSave is an easy means to saving. Save what you can - and gain instant access to your money when you need it.

Highlights

  • Ideal for casual saving - earn competitive interest rates on any balance
  • Move money to your account via stop order and access your funds whenever you like
  • Interest rates are variable, tiered by balance and are calculated daily and paid monthly
  • Link your PureSave account to your Standard Bank ATM card and manage your account online, at an ATM or via your cellphone

How to qualify

  • You are 16 years of age or older
    AND
  • You are a South African citizen
    OR
  • As a non-South African citizen, you have a valid passport, work permit or study permit
