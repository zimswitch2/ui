Your Cheque Card

Simpler day-to-day banking

Your Visa or MasterCard cheque card is linked to your current account and gives you a range of benefits that make day-to-day banking even simpler. With your cheque card you can make fast payments at merchants, easily withdraw cash, make deposits and transfers, and top up prepaid services at our AutoPlus ATMs. Use your cheque card anywhere you see Visa / MasterCard signs.
