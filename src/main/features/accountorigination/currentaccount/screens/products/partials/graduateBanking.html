Highlights

  • Apply for Prestige Banking, Private Banking or Elite Banking for qualified postgraduates
  • Access to a wide variety of finance options, including credit cards, overdraft facility, vehicle and asset finance, home loans, personal loans, study loans or business loans
  • Choose from a range of savings and investment products

How to qualify

  • You are over 18 years of age
  • You are a South African citizen or a permanent resident

    AND
  • You are a new customer and do not currently have a current account with Standard Bank

    OR
  • You have an E-plan Savings Account

    OR
  • You can upgrade your existing Student Achiever account

Additional information

Private Banking for young professionals

  • You may qualify to join Standard Bank Private Banking if you graduated from a South African University with a classic professional degree within the last 3 years - and are employed or training while being employed to become a chartered accountant, actuary, medical doctor or medical specialist, engineer, attorney or advocate

Prestige Banking for postgraduates

  • Our exclusive Titanium offering is available to individuals with a 3-year undergraduate degree with honours and who earn a minimum of R 5 000 a month

Elite Banking for graduates

  • You can apply for Elite Banking if you have a 3-year degree or diploma and earn a minimum of R 5 000 per month
