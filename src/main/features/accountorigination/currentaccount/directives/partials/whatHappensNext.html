Send your supporting documents

  • We need to verify your income. Please send us your last 3 payslips or bank statements
  • Print out and sign the overdraft agreement
  • Email all these documents to: supportingdocuments@standardbank.co.za
    Send as PDF, JPEG or TIFF. (Max file size: 4MB)

Confirming your application

  • You should receive an SMS within 1 working day after you apply
  • If you haven't received the SMS after 2 working days, please call 0860 123 000

Getting your card

  • A courier service will contact you to arrange delivery of your card
  • Delivery can take between 5 and 9 working days
  • Ensure you have your ID book with you when the courier arrives
  • Ensure you give copies of your ID and proof of home address to the courier

Linking your card

  • When you get your card, sign in to Internet banking and link it to your profile

Note that this overdraft offer is valid for 5 days. This offer constitutes approval in principle and is subject to the Bank verifying the information.
