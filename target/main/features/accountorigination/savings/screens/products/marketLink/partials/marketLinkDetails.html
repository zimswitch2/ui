MarketLink

With MarketLink you gain the flexibility of a current account in addition to competitive interest rates.

With MarketLink you gain the flexibility of a current account in addition to competitive interest rates.

Highlights

  • Cash held in your account is guaranteed
  • No defined investment period
  • Funds can be deposited and accessed at anytime
  • Stronger interest rates for investments of R 20 000 and more
  • Link your MarketLink account to your Standard Bank ATM card and manage your account online, at an ATM, or from your cell

How to qualify

  • You can submit an opening deposit of R 5 000
    AND
  • You are a South African citizen
    OR
  • As a non-South African citizen, you have a valid passport, work permit or study permit

Additional information

  • Interest is calculated on the daily balance and is paid into your MarketLink account on a monthly basis
  • You can create a weekly or monthly stop order from your current account to your MarketLink account
