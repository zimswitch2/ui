International Payment

You pay all the fees
You pay both the Standard Bank fees and the beneficiary’s bank fees. The beneficiary’s bank fees will be debited separately. Please make sure there are enough funds in your account.

Split the fees
You pay for the Standard Bank fees and the beneficiary will pay for their own bank fees.

Pricing
Commission fee: minimum R140 and maximum R640
(0.5% of transaction amount)
SWIFT fee: R100

Special pricing does not apply to international payments.
